Personal Information Collection and Privacy Policy Statements

Please read the following before you send us any Personal Data.

From time to time, it is necessary for clients to supply Sage with data in connection with their enquiries and our services.

Failure to supply such data may result in Sage being unable to reply the clients or provide or continue to provide services.

Data may be collected from clients in the ordinary course of the continuation of the business relationship between clients and Sage.

Providing personal information to Sage through electronic means (such as e-mail, Internet or voice recording system) cannot be guaranteed to be completely secure. Sage will not be responsible for any damage suffered by users if they send any information to Sage through electronic devices, or if Sage sends any information through electronic devices at the request of the user. Clients should take heed of such weaknesses and communicate personal information through electronic devices with caution.

Sage will not collect any personal information that identifies a visitor to this Website unless specified otherwise.

The purposes for which data relating to clients may be used (whether within or outside Hong Kong) are as follows:

  1. the daily operation of the services;
  2. conducting credit or reference checks;
  3. assisting other professional consultants involved to conduct credit checks;
  4. ensuring ongoing credit worthiness of clients;
  5. designing services or identifying related products/services for clients' use;
  6. marketing our services or related products;
  7. meeting the requirements to make disclosure under the requirements of any legal and/or regulatory requirements or court orders binding on Sage; and
  8. purposes relating to any of the above.

Use of Data in Direct Marketing:

Sage intends to use and/or transfer clients’ data to any related companies of Sage for direct marketing and Sage requires clients’ consent (which includes an indication of no objection) for that purpose. In this connection, please note that:

  1. Your name, contact details, products and services portfolio information, financial background and demographic data held by Sage from time to time may be used by Sage in direct marketing; and
  2. If a client does not wish Sage to use and/or transfer the client’s data for use in direct marketing, the client may, without charge, exercise the right to opt-out.

Data held by Sage relating to a client will be kept confidential but Sage may provide such information to the following parties (whether within or outside Hong Kong):

  1. any agent, consultant, contractor or third party service provider who provides professional, administrative, telecommunications, computer, financial, trade execution, cash, securities and/or contracts clearing or settlement or other services to Sage in connection with its services or the operation of its business;
  2. any other person under a duty of confidentiality to Sage including its subsidiaries (if any) or affiliated companies which have undertaken to keep such information confidential;
  3. any entity with which the client has or proposes to have dealings or businesses;
  4. any credit reference agency and in the event of default, any debt collection agency;
  5. any actual or proposed assignee of Sage or participant or sub-participant or transferee of the rights of Sage in respect of the client; and
  6. any exchange, entity, agency, regulatory or government body in any jurisdiction if required by law or pursuant to any court orders, rules or regulations to which Sage is subject. In such cases, Sage is usually under a duty of secrecy and will not be able to notify a client or seek his consent in relation to such release of information.

Under and in accordance with the terms of the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) (“Ordinance”), an individual has the right to:

  1. check whether Sage holds data about him and the right of access to such data;
  2. require Sage to correct any data relating to him which is inaccurate; and
  3. ascertain Sage's policies and practices in relation to data and to be informed of the kind of personal data held by Sage.
